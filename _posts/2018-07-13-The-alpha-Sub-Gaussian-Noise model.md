The wait is finally over! Find the Î±SGN(m) toolbox for Matlab at https://github.com/ahmd-mahm/alpha-SGNm. 

In 2015, we at the ARL (NUS) proposed the Î±SGN(m) model to characterize snapping shrimp noise. The model does not only characterize the impulsive statistics of the noise process, but its memory or "burstiness" as well.  Over time, we have developed several algorithms within the Î±SGN(m) framework. These were shown to improve upon performances of underwater acoustic systems operating in snapping shrimp noise.
